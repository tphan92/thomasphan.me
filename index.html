Projects - Informatics

Scaffold

For our senior capstone, my team and I created Scaffold. Scaffold is a quick and secure online platform that connect unskilled laborers with local employers.

Mobile App Design

In this Mobile Application Design class my teammates and I created a mobile design language. We then created a media player and health app with a specific design language.

For my computer science user experience class, my teammates and I created an application to fit a need. We created Relate, a mobile app for helping people maintain their relationships.
