Projects - Epic

Radar

Epic's dashboard application. Users can view graphs, tables, alerts, and other components that are tailored to them.

Reporting Workbench

Epic's report application. Users can view, subscribe, and favorite reports. Users can run and pend reports.

SlicerDicer

Epic's interactive research graphing appplication. Users can select columns from reports and graph the data real time.

Healthy Planet

Epic's accountable care and population managment suite of applications. Users can view reports and dashboards compiled from patient data.

Research Informatics

Epic's set of research tools. Users can manage patients health information, view patient history, and document adverse events.

Canto

Epic's tablet application. Users can access specific applications from Hyperspace (Epic's desktop client application suite) that is designed for tablets.

Star

Clarity

Predictive Analytics

Projects - Informatics

Scaffold

For our senior capstone, my team and I created Scaffold. Scaffold is a quick and secure online platform that connect unskilled laborers with local employers.

Mobile App Design

In this Mobile Application Design class my teammates and I created a mobile design language. We then created a media player and health app with a specific design language.

Relate

For my computer science user experience class, my teammates and I created an application to fit a need. We created Relate, a mobile app for helping people maintain their relationships.

Yellow Cab

For my Intro to UX class, my teammate and I were given the task to create a mobile application for Yellow Cab. The scenario involves Yellow Cab wanting to adopt a ride sharing model to keep up with new competitors.

Tess Rental

For my web programming, my teammate and I were given the task to find a business or person who needed a website. We created a website for Tess, a local Seattle home owner who runs her own home rental business.
